    Price Resolution Order

    When multiple rules match, the system resolves prices in this order:

    1. Agreement (vendor-specific pricing)
    2. Party (individual customer pricing)
    3. Organization (dive shop pricing)
    4. Global (default pricing)

    Within the same scope level, higher priority wins. If priority ties, most recent valid_from wins.
